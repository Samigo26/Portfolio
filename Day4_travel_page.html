Visit The Netherlands

The land of clogs, tulips, and canals.

Attractions

Keukenhof Field

These are the tulip fields near Amsterdam! They are very crowded usually and smell really good!

The Schiedam Jenever mills

Windmills are very common in the Netherlands, especially in the rural areas. Very pretty!

Van Gogh Museum

Van Gogh was a very famous dutch painter, and his museum is one of the most popular site in Amsterdam!

Food

Stroopwafels

Dutch stroopwafels are thin waffle cookies filled with caramel. They're popular with people of all nationalities, so I translated this Dutch recipe into English.

Gehakt Ballen

Dutch Meatballs are usually served with potatoes

Boterkoek

A Dutch buttery bar cookie, baked in a round tin, and cut into wedges after baking.
